Leavenworth, Washington

Pacific North West

A Bavarian-inspired town, Leavenworth is perfect all around the year. It’s a great spot for a weekend getaway from Seattle and it’s best to plan the trip in advance because of the number of incredible events that are held here throughout the year.

Seattle, Washington

Seattle, a city on Puget Sound in the Pacific Northwest, is surrounded by water, mountains and evergreen forests, and contains thousands of acres of parkland.

Mt. Rainier, Washington

Europe

One of the oldest national parks in the world, Mount Rainier National Park is set in Pierce County.
